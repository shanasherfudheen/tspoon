About Us

Welcome to Tspoon, where tradition meets taste. We specialize in offering authentic, high-quality curry powders and spice blends that bring the essence of Indian cooking into your kitchen. At Tspoon, we believe that the secret to delicious food lies in the ingredients. That’s why we carefully source premium spices and prepare them using traditional techniques — roasted, ground, and blended in small batches to preserve their natural flavor and aroma.

Our product range includes essentials like Garam Masala, Turmeric Powder, Chilli Powder, and Coriander Powder, along with regional favorites such as Fish Masala, Chicken Masala, Meat Masala, and Sambar Powder. We also offer unique staples like Rice Powder, Puttu Powder, and Kashmiri Chili Powder, making it easy to prepare authentic dishes from every part of India.

All our products are 100% natural, free from artificial colors and preservatives, and packed hygienically to retain freshness. Whether you're a home cook or a restaurant chef, our spices are crafted to deliver consistent quality and unforgettable taste. Tspoon was born out of a love for cooking and a passion for preserving traditional Indian flavors. With every pack, we aim to bring warmth, aroma, and authenticity to your meals.

Thank you for choosing Tspoon — your trusted partner in creating flavorful, wholesome, and memorable dishes.
